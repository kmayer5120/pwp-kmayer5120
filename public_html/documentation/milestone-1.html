Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:

The purpose of my personal website project is to be a page that promotes my wife's crochet business. This will be a page that she can share to others such that she can promote her crochet work, crochet patterns for sale, and to be contacted by buyers and potential pattern testers. The site serve as a brochure of sorts and will link to her Etsy, social media, and a Google form for potential pattern testers to apply to.

Audience:

The audience of this site will be crochet enthusiasts. Because this will be for artsy people, it will need to be aesthetically pleasing. There is a wide demographic for this hobby and people interested in it so it will need to be user friendly and easy to navigate. It is important that it is eye-catching and professional looking so the visitor will take my wife's crochet work and business seriously.

Goal:

The main goal is to drive visitors of the website to my wife's various online stores so they will buy her crochet products or buy her custom crochet patterns. The secondary goal is to drive visitors to her social media. The tertiary goal is to serve as a gallery for her crochet projects which should generate further interest in her business. Beyond these goals, this website will serve to show that I am capable of making a functional website for a business.
