Milestone 2

Content Strategy

The header will have my wife's company logo which we had designed by a graphic designer.
The navbar under the header will serve two purposes:
  • Link to parts of the page and scroll to them.
  • Link to external sites related to her business.

About Company Section

This about company section will have content introducing what my wife's crochet company does, sells, and any possible news. Because I'm making this for her real world use, I am having her write up a short description fill this section out. This should be relatively brief so as not to bore the user. There will also be some picture to catch the user's eye and direct there attention to this section. It will most likely either be another company logo or maybe another example of her work.

Gallery Section

This gallery section will serve to showcase her most recent work and patterns that she has completed. If any of these images are something for sale on her Etsy or Ravelry, then they will be linked to accordingly.

Links as SVG icons Section

This section will have icons as links to her external online stores any sort of social links.

Services Section

This section will talk about what services she offers: Custom crochet items, custom crochet patterns, etc. It will also serve to let the users know that she is accepting pattern testers and have a link to an external Google Form to apply for this. The Google Form has already been created and will only need to be linked to.

Contact Form Section

This section will serve as a means to make any sort of inquiries about products, services, or to learn more about becoming a pattern tester. The user will input their name, email address, and a brief message. The front end will pass this information to the backend, compose an email, and finally send that email directly to her.
